Customer Behaviour SQL Analysis

"Where is this business actually making money and who are the customers driving it?"

Analysed 780,000+ real UK e-commerce transactions across 4 marketing frameworks: RFM segmentation, cohort retention, market basket analysis, and revenue trends. Found that 600 customers (10% of the base) drive 49% of £9.7M revenue. Identified a 65-80% first-purchase churn rate and a consistent September revenue spike of 38-48% MoM.

Airbnb Albany Pricing EDA

"What actually drives Airbnb pricing and is it what everyone assumes?"

Exploratory data analysis on 426 real Airbnb listings. Challenged the assumption that review scores drive pricing correlation was just 0.09. The real driver? Capacity (0.53). Engineered a price_per_person metric that completely reversed the perceived value rankings of luxury listings.
